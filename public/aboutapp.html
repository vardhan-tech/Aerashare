About Aerashare

Aerashare makes file sharing fast, secure, and effortless — and best of all, it’s completely free. There are no limits or hidden restrictions on how much you can share. Whether a few megabytes or hundreds of gigabytes, your transfers happen smoothly and directly in your browser — no downloads, installations, or sign-ups required. Our goal is simple: to give users a powerful sharing experience without complexity or compromise. Just open the site, select your files, and share instantly — it’s that easy. Your privacy and security come first. None of your files or personal data are stored on our servers — every transfer is processed securely and removed automatically. We don’t track, sell, or keep any of your information. What you share belongs entirely to you.
